How can a project manager help you?

We help plan your project.

Planning your remodeling or building project is an essential and crucial part of any residential project. We set proper goals and expectations about what can be delivered, by when and for how much. We negotiate reasonable and achievable deadlines with milestones shared among homeowners and trades. Before the project kick-off, we will create and help you:

Here are five key skills project manager bring to the table.

#organization #communication #budgeting #knowledge #experience
#organization

We organize your project.

We organize your kitchen, bath, basements, addition projects into phases and task lists and assign tasks to trades and monitor the project to make sure we are on time and under budget. We organize all trades in a manner that they are in sync with other trades for a smooth transition. We pride ourselves at juggling different deadlines, tasks, risks and QA issues – so you don’t have to.

#communication

We communicate your goals.

One of the most critical steps in your remodeling/construction project is to ensure that communication lines are open. Most homeowners are not well versed in construction processes enough to understand trades languages. We enable confident, clear and concise communication within the trades and homeowner.
This helps eliminate grey areas, decreases confusion and increases accuracy.

#budgeting

Time and cost management.

The number one reason most homeowners hire us it so effectively mange time and budget. We negotiate reasonable and achievable deadlines and milestones among construction trades and homeowners. Too often, most contractors are looking out for themselves and end up compromising the necessary steps, and ultimately, the quality of a project’s outcome.

Having us on board will ensure that you will be informed immediately about how any changes will affect the project timeline and budget.

#knowledge

Our knowledge is control.

We are control freaks and that is where most homeowners need us the most because we are continuously measuring, checking and correcting throughout the project. This is important because it ensures the quality of the project is not compromised. Not only are we concerned about the quality, we also make sure applicable local building codes are met.

#experience

We are a wealth of experience.

Our experience is drawn from past successes and failures to make sure mistakes won’t happen on your project. We understand the importance of and are able to visualize while still able to focus on the minute details of day-to-day tasks. It may be tempting to cut out the project manager to save a few dollars however having a competent project manager overseeing your next project will most likely save you time, money and headaches!
